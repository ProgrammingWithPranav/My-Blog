Super Rolling Ball

Wanna have fun and control a rolling ball? Super Rolling Ball is a game made in unity by Pranav Tantri. You have to control the ball and get to the goalpost as fast as you can. Remember, the faster you get to the goal, the more coins you get. Coins can be used to buy skins for the player.

Mini Planets

Mini Planets

Can you save little Mini Planets from colliding with each other? Mini Planets is a game in which you have to save tiny planets from colliding with each other. Some planets are green, kind and slow, while the others are red, rude and furious. How To Play? Well, it's simple. Drag the planets away from each other with your finger and stop them from colliding with each other. There is time timer on the top right corner. Stop the planets from touching each other until the time reaches 0.
